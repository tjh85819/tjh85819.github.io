Atlanta Hawks

Welcome to the Atlanta Hawks Page!

The Hawks are a professional basketball team based in Atlanta. The team competes in the league's Eastern Conference Southeast Division. The Hawks currently hold the second-longest drought of not winning an NBA championship at 57 seasons but are looking to change that as the team's roster has become better with each season.

Lasting over 5 decades, the Hawks have a long-time rivlary with the Boston Celtics. These two teams have faced each other over 11 times in the NBA Playoffs and 4 times in the NBA Finals. This rivalry only deepened when the Hawks traded All-Star Center Al Horford to the Celtics.

Next summer, renonvations for the Hawk's home court, Philips Arena, will begin. The overhaul will cost a total of $192.5 million. As part of this deal, the Hawks signed an 18-year lease extension to remain at the city-owned arena through 2046. The reonovations should be complete in time for the start of the 2018-19 season.
